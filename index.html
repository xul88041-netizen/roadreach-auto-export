FAQ

Before you inquire

Can you quote CIF to my country?+

Yes. Send your destination country and nearest seaport. We will confirm available shipping routes and quote the vehicle plus estimated freight.

How do I verify the vehicle condition?+

Request current photos, video and available inspection information for the specific unit before placing an order.

Can I order more than one vehicle?+

Yes. Dealers and fleet buyers can request multiple units and container-loading options where practical.

Which documents are supplied?+

Documents depend on the vehicle and destination, but typically include the commercial invoice and export/shipping paperwork needed for import clearance.
